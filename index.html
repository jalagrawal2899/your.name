About

Your Name is a critically acclaimed anime film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It tells the story of Mitsuha and Taki, two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies and embark on a journey to unravel the truth behind their connection.

Characters

Mitsuha

Mitsuha is a high school girl living in a rural town. She yearns for a more exciting life in Tokyo and dreams of becoming a stylish, modern girl.

Taki

Taki is a high school boy living in Tokyo. He has a part-time job and is focused on his studies. Despite leading a busy life, he experiences strange dreams of being in a rural town.
